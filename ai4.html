Applications of Artificial Intelligence

Healthcare

AI helps in disease diagnosis, drug discovery, personalized treatment, and robotic surgeries.

Finance

Used for fraud detection, risk management, automated trading, and customer service chatbots.

Transportation

Autonomous vehicles, traffic management, and route optimization use AI to improve safety and efficiency.

Education

AI provides personalized learning experiences, automates grading, and offers virtual tutors.

Entertainment

Recommendation systems for music, movies, and games are powered by AI.
