Challenges of Artificial Intelligence

  • Job Displacement: Automation may replace some human jobs.
  • Privacy Issues: AI systems often need large amounts of personal data.
  • Bias: AI can inherit biases present in training data.
  • Security Risks: AI could be used maliciously or hacked.
  • Complexity: Designing and understanding AI systems is challenging.
  • Ethical Concerns: Decisions made by AI can raise moral questions.
